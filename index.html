Your New Travel Planner and Guide Map App

Got a things to do and places to see list ready for your next trip? Of course you do.
But wouldn't it be handy if you could easily see all those destinations with tips on a tip map
AND see how far the places are relative to wherever you are? Well even if you didn't,
you can still use it as a travel guide and visit the best places travel Youtubers and more recommend.

That's the beauty of the Tip Map App, your easy to use on-the-go travel planner.

Top 10 Benefits of Tip Map

  • Easy to use and straight to the point.
  • Save must-see and must-eat destinations before your trip.
  • Save them randomly or deliberately on an easy to navigate map app.
  • Quickly add recommendations from your favorite influencers.
  • Save notes and view tips for destinations.
  • Can be used alongside more complex travel apps and travel plans
  • Fill in empty days and lulls with amazing place recommendations.
  • See how far all destinations are from your hotel or location.
  • See Top 10 things to do list as recommended by travel influencers.
  • Support your favorite Youtubers, Instagram Users, travel bloggers, and more.

About Tip Map

Hi, I got the idea for Tip Map during my first visit to Japan. With so many places to go and see, and eat and drink, I was overwhelmed. I had notes and recommendations everywhere. A lot of my trip plans came from watching Facebook videos, Youtube videos, and researching. It took a lot of time but a lot of those places were highlights of my trip.

There were even times I visited the place and forgot what the travel influencer recommended so I had to pull up the Youtube video and search for it. I hope this app solves those problems and highlights the best places travel influencers recommend in a simple, easy to use way.

Since I’ve started working on it, I’ve been continuously coming up with more features to help make it the most comprehensive trip map app out there but do need extra dev help. I want to make it as simple to use and as useful as possible. So, if you think, yes, Tip Map sounds like something you’d use, or if you’re an influencer in the travel sphere who’ll be interested in featuring your recommendations on the app, please reach out so I know to continue. Thank you.
