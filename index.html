Tip Map

Simple travel app where you can save interesting points found before your trip
and view them on a map during your trip. Featured page where you can tip
your favorite influencers, view and add places they visited/recommend
to your folder, and help create a map focused recommendation list

  • Learn more

Before the Trip

Instead of having to jot down all the names of interesting points you want to visit on Apple or Android notes, this app aims to be a simple journey planner that allows you to store, keep notes, and see markers on a location map whenever you come across interesting restauraunts and non-restauraunts in Youtube, Facebook or Instagram posts.

  • Learn more

During the Trip

With all the interesting places added to each collection over time, and ideally with the hotel being in the center of the city and points, you can then open each collection and get a grasp of the places you can visit based on distance and convenience according to the map in your hotel room at night. Maybe even get directions during the day to see what places you did not think were close by for those spontaneous wanderers.
